Welcome to my version of Map Mania!

In this game, you are tasked with finding my favorite places on the planet! Your first hint, is that there are 10 of them.

You will look around the globe, gaining points when you find a location at a certain zoom level, at which point you will be reset back to the original position in order to look for the next one.

Do not be overwhelmed! There are hints in place to make your job a little easier.

Thank you for playing, and good luck!
