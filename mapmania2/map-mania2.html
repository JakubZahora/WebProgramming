Welcome to my version of Map Mania!

In this game, you are tasked with finding my favorite places on the planet! Your first hint, is that there are 10 of them.

You will look around the globe, gaining points when you find a location at a certain zoom level (10), at which point you will be reset back to the original position in order to look for the next one.

Do not be overwhelmed! There are hints in place to make your job a little easier. Pay attention to the color of the background!

If feeling defeated, double clicking "Can you find them all?" at the top of the screen will win the game for you.

Thank you for playing!
